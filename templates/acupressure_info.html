What is Acupressure/Acupuncture?

Acupressure and acupuncture are traditional Chinese medicine techniques that stimulate specific points on the body to promote healing and relieve pain.

While acupuncture uses thin needles, acupressure applies pressure with fingers, hands, or special devices to the same points.

Benefits

Effective pain management
Restores energy flow (Qi)
Reduces stress and anxiety
Improves sleep quality
Enhances immune function

Common Conditions Treated

  • Chronic pain (back, neck, joints)
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Arthritis and fibromyalgia
  • Digestive disorders
  • Respiratory conditions
  • Anxiety and depression
  • Insomnia and sleep disorders
  • Menstrual and menopausal symptoms

Treatment Process

1. Consultation

Initial assessment, health history, and pulse diagnosis

2. Diagnosis

Identification of energy imbalances and acupoints

3. Treatment

Pressure application or needle insertion at specific points

4. Duration

30-60 minute sessions, typically 6-12 sessions for chronic conditions

What is Homeopathy?

Homeopathy is a natural system of medicine that uses highly diluted substances to trigger the body's own healing mechanisms.

Based on the principle of "like cures like," it treats individuals with minute doses of substances that would produce similar symptoms in healthy people.

Benefits

Gentle treatment with minimal side effects
Individualized care based on symptoms
Safe for all ages including infants
Can be used alongside conventional medicine
Addresses root cause of illness

Common Conditions Treated

  • Allergies and hay fever
  • Respiratory infections
  • Skin conditions like eczema
  • Digestive disorders
  • Migraines and headaches
  • Anxiety and depression
  • Arthritis and joint pain
  • Hormonal imbalances

Treatment Process

1. Detailed Consultation

In-depth discussion of symptoms, emotions, and lifestyle

2. Remedy Selection

Matching symptoms to appropriate homeopathic remedy

3. Prescription

Highly diluted remedies in pellet or liquid form
