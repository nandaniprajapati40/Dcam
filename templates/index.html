About Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya

A unique university established with the vision of synthesizing the spiritual values of the East with the scientific temper of the West.

Department of Complementary & Alternative Medicine (DCAM)

Department History

March 2007

Established as 'Yog Arogya Polyclinic' under the guidance and patronage of Hon'ble Chancellor, Dr Pranav Pandya

March 2016

Name changed to 'Centre of Complementary & Alternative Medicine' (CCAM)

May 2018

Formed as a Department and given its current name 'The Department of Complementary & Alternative Medicine' (DCAM)

Location

The department is situated in natural and spiritual ambience on the ground floor of Chaitanya Bhavan & near Yagyashala of Dev Sanskriti University, Gayatrikunj- Shantikunj, Haridwar (Bharat).

Vision

"The Department has a vision to establish as a leader in academic excellence, innovation, and community impact in the field of Complementary and Alternative Medicine, fostering a holistic approach to global health and well-being."

Mission

  • To deliver education and training in Complementary and Alternative Medicine with science and spirituality.
  • To promote an interdisciplinary approach to CAM education and practice.
  • To contribute to community health by providing accessible and effective CAM-based healthcare solutions.
  • To cultivate future leaders in the field of CAM with a deep sense of human values and universal brotherhood.
