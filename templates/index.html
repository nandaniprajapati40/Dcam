About Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya

Our Vision

Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya (DSVV) is a unique university established with the vision of integrating modern education with ancient Indian wisdom and spiritual values. The university aims to create a harmonious blend of scientific temperament and spiritual consciousness.

DCAM Department

The Department of Complementary and Alternative Medicines (DCAM) at DSVV is dedicated to research, education, and practice of traditional healing systems. Our mission is to revive and promote ancient healing wisdom while integrating it with contemporary healthcare approaches.

Key Focus Areas

  • Research in traditional healing systems
  • Integration of ancient and modern therapies
  • Holistic health education and training
  • Community wellness programs
  • Scientific validation of traditional practices
